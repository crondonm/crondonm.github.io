Carlos Rondón-Moreno

Senior Economist · Central Bank of Chile · Research Department

Ph.D. in Economics, University of Notre Dame (2019). B.A. & M.A., Universidad de los Andes (Bogotá). I work on macroprudential policy and the anatomy of financial crises in small open economies — capital controls, sudden stops, fiscal consolidation — with a focus on computational methods, Python, and ML/AI methods (mostly JAX) for economic modelling.

Recent papers

  • Debt and Taxes: Optimal Fiscal Consolidation in the Small Open Economy — Central Bank of Chile WP №941, 2022.
  • Tracking R of COVID-19: A New Real-time Estimation Using the Kalman Filter — with Arroyo-Marioli, Bullano, Kucinskas. PLoS ONE 16(1): e0244474, 2021.
